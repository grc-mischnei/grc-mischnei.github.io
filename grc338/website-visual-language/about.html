Welcome to our Coffee and Tea shop located in Seattle, Washington!

Our mission is to provide our customers with a unique and delicious food experience in a warm and inviting atmosphere. We offer a wide variety of specialty coffee and tea drinks, as well as our signature pastries made with locally-sourced ingredients.

Our coffee is freshly roasted and expertly brewed to deliver the perfect cup every time. Whether you prefer a bold espresso shot or a creamy latte, we have something for every taste bud.

Our tea selection includes a variety of loose-leaf blends from around the world, ranging from classic black tea to exotic herbal infusions. We also offer a selection of iced teas for those hot Seattle summer days.

In addition to our menu items, we provide a comfortable and inviting space for our customers to enjoy their drinks and food. We pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability, using eco-friendly products and minimizing waste.

We are also dedicated to supporting our local community by sourcing our ingredients from local farms and businesses. We believe in creating a positive impact on our environment and society, one cup at a time.

Thank you for choosing Seattle Roasters. We can't wait to serve you!
